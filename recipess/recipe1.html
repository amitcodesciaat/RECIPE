Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Home
  • Categories

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 Eggs
  • 50g Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Mix eggs and cheese in a bowl.
  4. Toss spaghetti with pancetta and remove from heat.
  5. Add the egg mixture and stir quickly.
  6. Serve with extra cheese and black pepper.
